About Panera Bread

Our Origins

Panera Bread began in 1987 as a small community bakery under the name St. Louis Bread Company, founded with a secret sourdough starter from San Francisco and a belief that the best part of bread is sharing it.

Continuing to Evolve — “The New Era”

In 2024, Panera launched its biggest menu overhaul yet: more than 20 new and enhanced offerings, focusing on what guests love most — soups, salads, sandwiches, mac & cheese, bakery items, and coffee & drinks.

The revamp reflects our commitment to fresh ideas, quality ingredients, variety, and value — staying true to our roots while evolving to meet modern tastes and lifestyles.

Our Promise

At Panera Bread, we believe nothing beats breaking bread together. Whether it’s a quick lunch, a cozy breakfast, or a gathering with friends, we’re committed to serving food that feels good in the moment — and long after.
